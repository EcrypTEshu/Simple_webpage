Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite food.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium hear.
  2. gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once butter has melted add cream and eggs.
  4. using a spatula fold fold the eggs from the egde of the pan to the center every 20 second.
  5. when the eegs are still remove from the heat.
